# LA County City Managers Database

A public resource tracking leadership updates across all 88 cities in Los Angeles County.

## Purpose
- Share timely updates on City Manager appointments, transitions, and interim service
- Support professional local government management
- Advocate for the council–manager form of government

## Live site
After you enable GitHub Pages (Settings → Pages), your site will appear at:
`https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/YOURREPO/`

## Files to add
1. Put your dataset here: `data/la_county_city_managers.csv`
2. Put your logo here: `assets/logo.png`

## Notes
- Column **N** is hidden by default (14th column).
- To hide a different column, edit `HIDE_COLUMN_INDEX` in `index.html`.
